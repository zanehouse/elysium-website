Company Insiders — source: SEC EDGAR, Form 4 filings only (officers, directors, and 10% owners). Covered names carry full transaction detail (insider, type, shares, price), parsed and refreshed periodically; any other ticker loads live from EDGAR as a filing list with links — the SEC does not permit browsers to read the transaction XML directly, so detail for uncovered tickers opens on EDGAR. Transaction codes: P buy, S sell, A grant, F tax withholding, M option exercise, G gift. Congress — source: the official House Clerk financial-disclosure index, Periodic Transaction Reports (stock trades) only. The House publishes no machine-readable transaction detail — the linked PDF report is the source of truth for what was bought or sold. Senate disclosures aren't covered here; see efdsearch.senate.gov. Filings are delayed by SEC/House reporting deadlines. Not investment advice.
