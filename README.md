# Elysium Capital

**Independent Macro Research & Market Intelligence**

Elysium Capital is a self-directed research platform focused on analyzing macroeconomic regimes, cross-asset relationships, and structural market forces. The project aims to translate complex economic data into clear, actionable insights for traders and investors.

---

## Overview

This site serves as a central hub for:

* Macro research and written analysis
* Framework development (regime models, indicators, signals)
* Ongoing exploration of global financial systems

The goal is to build a structured understanding of how liquidity, rates, inflation, and credit interact across market cycles.

---

## Core Focus Areas

* **Macroeconomic Regimes**
  Identifying risk-on / risk-off environments using data-driven frameworks

* **Interest Rates & Liquidity**
  Analysis of bond yields, central bank policy, and systemic liquidity

* **Cross-Asset Dynamics**
  Relationships between equities, bonds, commodities, and currencies

* **Market Structure**
  Applying quantitative and discretionary models to real market conditions
